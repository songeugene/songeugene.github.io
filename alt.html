ALT Data Visualization


A website exploring audio features and release dates of GRAMMY and Billboard charting songs.

Eugene Song • 3 min read


role :

Research, Information Design, Data Visualization, Visual Design, Web Design

team :

Christie Chen, Isabel Silverman, Eugene Song (me)

duration :

11.2021 – 12.2021 (6 weeks)

tools :

Figma, d3.js, HTML/CSS




Background


Our main motivation was to better illustrate the various trends in the music industry and compile relevant data into one visualization. During our research for the project, we realized that it would be useful to have more visualizations for musical data that analyze information from multiple sources; we thought that this visualization could be useful for those who are interested in music news and upcoming trends. Furthermore, aspiring musicians and audio producers interested in winning GRAMMY awards may find our visualizations useful in finding out how to cater their creations to the voting members of the GRAMMY committee – both in track features and in release date. Music also plays a huge role in social media trends, such as on TikTok. Analyzing these audio features could help in predicting which songs would go viral.



Objective


The main objective for this project was to visualize trends in audio features throughout the years or across categories in the GRAMMYs awards. This includes creating a series of visualizations that clearly display 1) musical qualities of award-winning songs both throughout the years in a single category and throughout categories in a single year and 2) possible trends in GRAMMY-winning songs and their release dates. Our visualization would directly relate details on the audio features for songs based on a reliable data source to the type of category or year in which the song won the GRAMMY, allowing users to directly compare trends and track features that make for a successful song in the voting committee. Users will also be able to see data on how the release date could be a factor in influencing the chances the song will win a GRAMMY.
